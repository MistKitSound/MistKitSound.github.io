    License

    1. Non Commercial License

    By purchasing this license, you are granted the right to use the music track for non-commercial purposes only. You are allowed to:
    • Use the track in personal projects
    • Use the track in videos that are not monetized
    • Share content with the track on social media (without monetization)
    You are NOT allowed to:
    • Use the track in commercial projects
    • Monetize videos or content containing the track
    • Resell, redistribute, or claim the track as your own
    The track remains the property of MistKit. Credit is required: "Music by MistKit"

    2. Commercial License (NON EXCLUSIVE)

    By purchasing this license, you are granted a non-exclusive right to use the music track in commercial projects. You are allowed to:
    • Use the track in monetized YouTube videos
    • Use the track in games, apps, and films
    • Use the track in client projects
    You are NOT allowed to:
    • Resell, redistribute, or sublicense the track
    • Claim ownership of the track
    Important:
    • This is a non-exclusive license
    • The track may be sold to multiple customers
    • Existing licenses remain valid even if exclusive rights are sold later
    The track remains the property of MistKit. Credit is appreciated but not required.

    3. Full Rights (EXCLUSIVE)

    By purchasing this license, you obtain exclusive rights to the music track. You are granted:
    • Full commercial use without limitations
    • The right to modify, edit, and use the track in any project
    • Exclusive ownership of usage rights

    Important:

    • The track will be removed from sale after purchase
    • It will not be sold or licensed to new customers

    Note:

    • Previous buyers with non-exclusive licenses retain their rights

    You are NOT allowed to:

    • Claim authorship of the track (MistKit remains the original creator unless agreed otherwise)

    This agreement grants exclusive usage rights, not authorship transfer.
